Ingredients

  • Maize flour
  • water
  • Chicken
  • Tomatoes
  • Cooking oil
  • Curry powder, onion, salt, pepper

Procedure

  1. Bring the water to a boil in a sufuria.
  2. Gradually add maize flour while stirring to avoid lumps.
  3. Keep stirring until thickened and it pulls away from the pot sides.
  4. Keep stirring until thickened and it pulls away from the sufuria sides.
  5. Heat oil in a pan, sauté onions until soft.
  6. Add garlic, ginger, and spices. Stir for 1–2 minutes
  7. Add chicken pieces and cook until browned.
  8. Add tomatoes and cook until soft.
  9. Add a little water, cover, and simmer until chicken is tender.
  10. Garnish with coriander and serve with ugali.

Nutritional Info

~430–530 kcal, 28g protein, 40g carbs, 15g fat
