Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Rice
  • pilau spices
  • water,oil, ginger ,garlic , onion, salt, pepper

Procedure

  1. Brown beef and set aside
  2. Sauté onions until browned; add garlic, ginger, spices.
  3. Add tomatoes, cook until soft
  4. Return beef, add rice and water (1:2 ratio), simmer until cooked.

Nutritional Info

~500–650 kcal, 25–30g protein, 50g carbs, 20g fat
